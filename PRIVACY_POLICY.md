# Privacy Policy for Screentation

**Last Updated: July 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **Screentation** ("we," "our," or "the Application") handles information when you use the software. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Screentation is a desktop-based utility designed to capture, annotate, and export screenshots. 

---

## 1. No Data Collection and Transmission
Screentation is a fully offline application. 
* **No Telemetry**: We do not collect, monitor, or store any personal data, usage telemetry, or statistics.
* **No Server Connectivity**: The Application does not connect to any external databases or remote servers. All operations are executed locally on your computer.
* **No Third-Party Analytics**: No third-party tracking, advertising SDKs, or analytics tools are integrated into the Application.

## 2. Screenshot and Image Data
* **Local Storage**: All captured screenshots, crops, and annotated images are processed in your computer's RAM and saved exclusively to the directory on your local storage that you select in the settings.
* **No Uploads**: Your captured screenshots and drawings are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted to us or any third parties.

## 3. Clipboard Access
* Screentation monitors the system clipboard to detect when an image is copied (such as when you press PrintScreen or use the Snipping Tool).
* This clipboard checking is performed entirely on your local machine. No clipboard contents are read or processed unless they contain image data, and no clipboard information is ever sent over the network.

## 4. Local Settings
* The Application saves your preferences (such as default export folders, color selections, and file formats) in a local JSON configuration file located in your user profile folder (`%LocalAppData%\Screentation`). This configuration remains entirely on your machine.
